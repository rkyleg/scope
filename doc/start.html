Getting Started

  • New - Click the new button and select the language environment.
  • Open - Click the open button or drag and drop files to the tab bar to open them.

Settings

  • Settings are stored in settings.yaml. Be careful modifying it and watch out for spaces as they matter with YAML.
  • A settings interface is planned for future development

afide is still in early development and has not been appropriately tested. use at your own risk.
